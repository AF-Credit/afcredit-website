Who we are

A direct lender built for
people who need to move fast.

AF Credit is a specialist principal bridging lender. We lend our own capital, make decisions in-house, and work directly with borrowers and intermediaries to complete quickly and with certainty.

Our story

Built on frustration
with the status quo.

The bridging market has grown — but it has also grown complicated. Layers of review, external credit committees and slow processes have pushed average completion times to 40–50 days.

AF Credit was founded to offer something different: a lender that makes its own decisions, communicates directly, and completes quickly without cutting corners on due diligence.

We lend across England and Wales on residential, commercial and mixed-use property — from straightforward chain-break loans to more complex commercial scenarios.
